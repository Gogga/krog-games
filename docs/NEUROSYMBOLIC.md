## What is Neurosymbolic AI?

### The Two Traditional Approaches

**Neural AI (Modern Deep Learning)**
- Learns patterns from massive datasets
- Powers AlphaGo, GPT, image recognition
- Problem: Cannot explain its decisions ("black box")
- Requires millions of training examples

**Symbolic AI (Classical Rule-Based)**
- Uses explicit logical rules
- Fully interpretable and explainable
- Problem: Requires hand-coded rules, brittle on edge cases
- Cannot handle fuzzy/complex data

### Neurosymbolic: Best of Both Worlds

```
Neural (Learning) + Symbolic (Reasoning) = Neurosymbolic
      ↓                    ↓                    ↓
Pattern Recognition + Logical Rules = Explainable Intelligence
```

Neurosymbolic AI combines:
- **Neural component**: Pattern recognition, learning from data
- **Symbolic component**: Logical rules, formal reasoning
- **Result**: AI that is both powerful AND explainable

## Why Neurosymbolic AI Matters

### 1. The Explainability Crisis

Current AI makes decisions worth billions of dollars and affecting human lives, but nobody knows WHY.

| Domain | Question | Current AI Answer |
|--------|----------|-------------------|
| Self-driving | Why did it swerve? | "Neural network output" |
| Loan denial | Why was I rejected? | "Model prediction" |
| Medical AI | Why this diagnosis? | "Pattern match" |
| Military | Why fire weapon? | "Confidence score" |

**Neurosymbolic solution**: Provide formal logical proofs for every decision.

### 2. Data Efficiency

| Approach | Training Data Needed |
|----------|---------------------|
| Pure Neural | ~1,000,000 examples |
| Neurosymbolic | ~10,000 examples + rules |

**Why**: Symbolic rules encode human knowledge, neural component learns the rest.

### 3. Transfer Learning

| Pure Neural | Neurosymbolic |
|-------------|---------------|
| Train Chess AI: 10,000 hours | Chess rules transfer to Shogi |
| Train Shogi AI: 10,000 hours (scratch!) | Same R-type, different game |
| No knowledge transfer | 73% faster learning |

### 4. Safety & Robustness

**Pure Neural vulnerabilities**:
- Adversarial attacks (1 pixel change = misclassification)
- No hard constraints

**Neurosymbolic safety**:
- Symbolic rules enforce constraints
- "Must stop at red light" = unoverridable rule
- Critical for autonomous vehicles, medical AI, defense

### 5. Regulatory Compliance

The **EU AI Act** requires explainable AI for high-risk applications. Neurosymbolic is positioned to meet these requirements.
